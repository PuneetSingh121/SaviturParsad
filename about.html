BIO

Savitur Prasad was born on 25th July 1958 in Gurdaspur, Punjab, graduated in Economics Honours from University of Delhi, postgraduated in Philosophy from University of Delhi, with a Master of Science (MSc) in Defence Studies from Defence Services Staff College, Wellington. He joined the Indian Defence Accounts Service in 1983. He has served on various important assignments in Central and State Govt. in last 35 years of service and formulated policies which had national ramifications with visible impact.
